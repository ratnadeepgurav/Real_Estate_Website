Agriculture Irrigation

Irrigation is the process of supplying water artificially to the land, there are many irrigation systems that requires water pump as it plays important role in lifting the water. Water pump is one of main invention used for water pumping purpose and can assist in efficient irrigation like agriculture, lawn maintenance etc.

Agriculture is primary source of focus for Indian farmers and there are lots of issues that farmers come across like lack of water resource, lack of electricity etc. As we all know that agriculture irrigation is the process of transferring water to the soil that can enhance agriculture productivity at lower cost. So, the efficient agriculture irrigation requires a continuous water supply and agriculture pump are designed and integrated to fulfill this need as a part of agriculture irrigation system.
